Sleep Over with Auntie

Anyways, we stayed up sooo late last night! I thought Mom went to bed early but actually Auntie goes to bed way earlier its crazy. Leer had to keep walking on her in order to get breakfast, we were dying that’s how hungry we were! For breakfast we had these healthy chimkens that are not as good as normal chimkens. I might have eaten some of Cat’s chimkens instead, but don’t say anything!

Ok bai!
